## 6. General Discriminants for Future ISOs

This protocol defines **what would count as genuinely suspicious** in future objects:

1. **Excess NGA beyond volatile limits**
   - Measured acceleration cannot be matched by any plausible outgassing given observed coma and thermophysical limits.

2. **Non-thermal, discrete maneuvers**
   - Sudden Œîv events, plane changes, or thrust episodes *not* correlated with solar heating or dust outbursts.

3. **Isotopic or elemental fingerprints suggestive of industry**
   - Clear signatures of refined alloys, non-solar isotopic ratios, or reactor products.

4. **Engineered radio/optical signals**
   - Narrowband, modulated, or patterned emission spatially and temporally coincident with the ISO.

5. **Coherent swarms / formation flying**
   - Multiple objects with tightly correlated trajectories that cannot be explained as fragments or dust.

6. **Population-level anomalies**
   - A recurring sub-population of ISOs whose behaviors systematically defy natural models.

When describing these, the assistant should mark them as **hypothetical discriminants**, not current evidence.
